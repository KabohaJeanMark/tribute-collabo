Pele

The man who made football known as the beautiful game

Below is a timeline of his life

  • 1940 Pele was born in October.
  • 1956 Pele joined the club Santos.
  • 1958 The 17 year old was phenomenal winning his first world cup with Brazil.
  • 1962 He scored some good goals and assists. But was injured. Brazil won the world cup again.
  • 1966 Pele was a victim to malicious tackles and got very injured. Brazil lost.
  • 1970 Pele was part of the best goal scored in that century. He won his third world cup.
